What Others Are Saying About Us:

Review of Esteemed

As someone who has has been fortunate to be part of several of Jamie Hulker’s Bible Studies, I can say that the The Esteemed Study was one of my favorites. Jamie’s knowledge and research are evident in this insightful and in-depth exposition of strong female women during biblical times. She presents the information in ways that are relatable and easy to understand but that are grounded in scripture.

Although some of the stories are familiar, Jamie challenged the group to put aside preconceived ideas and focus solely on scripture. There were several women included in the study that I was not familiar with and found their stories to be powerful and encouraging. I gained valuable insight into some incredible women that God esteemed.

Whether you are wanting to deepen your knowledge of the Bible, needing encouragement or just looking for food for thought, I cannot recommend this engaging study enough. It definitely encouraged to me and gave me a fresh perspective of how God sees me and my role in His kingdom.

-Crystal

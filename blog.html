Encouraging: New Life and Blogs

“Having so fond an affection for you, we were well-pleased to impart to you not only the gospel of God but also our own lives…” (1 Thess. 1:8a NASB 95)

At Jamie Hulker: Here for You we hope to encourage you by sharing what God’s doing in our lives and hearing about what He’s doing in yours. We believe that walking through life together is such a vital part of following Jesus. Seeing how God’s working in others—what He’s teaching them and how He’s showing up—helps us all grow deeper in understanding His love. While we hope you’re plugged into your local church, we know that’s not always easy, so we invite you to be part of our community as well!

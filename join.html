Engaging: Study Tours and Ministry Connections

“For the earth will be filled with the knowledge of the glory of the Lord, as the waters cover the sea." (Habakkuk 2:14, NASB 95)

Jamie Hulker: Here for You isn’t just about studying God’s Word—it’s about engaging with the world around us. We offer life-changing opportunities, which we like to call uncommon journeys, for you to experience the places where the stories of the Bible took place; and we offer opportunities for you to make a meaningful difference in the world by serving God as you serve others.

We’d love for you to join us on any or all of our upcoming uncommon journeys listed below!

At Jamie Hulker: Here for You, we believe that studying God’s Word is only the beginning. Our mission also includes encouraging you to engage with both Scripture and the world around you in meaningful, transformative ways for yourself and others. Through these life-changing experiences, which we call uncommon journeys, you’ll have the opportunity to walk the lands where the stories of the Bible unfolded and see the Scriptures come to life in new ways. In addition, we offer opportunities to serve God by serving others, encouraging you to make a lasting impact on the lives of those around you. We invite you to explore our upcoming uncommon journeys here and take the next step in your spiritual adventure!

The Uncommon Journey

At Jamie Hulker: Here for You, we believe in more than just studying Scripture—we’re passionate about engaging with the world around us. Through our uncommon journeys, you’ll experience the biblical lands firsthand and have the chance to serve God by serving others. Our journeys are divided into two categories: Walking in the Footsteps, where you can walk the paths of the Bible, experiencing key biblical sites, and Walking Alongside, which focuses on serving others while engaging in meaningful mission opportunities. Join us for an unforgettable journey that connects you with both Scripture and the world in impactful ways! Explore our upcoming journeys below.

Walking in the Footsteps: Bible Study Tours

Step into the pages of Scripture and watch the Bible come to life! Walking in the Footsteps takes you to the heart of where it all happened—immersing you in the landscapes of biblical events with the insight of local experts and our passionate study tour leaders. These unforgettable journeys will transform how you see and understand God's Word. Ready to take your faith deeper? Explore the details of our study tours below then join the adventure! jamie@theuncommonjourney.net!

An Uncommon Journey to Israel

Embark on an unforgettable journey where the Bible comes alive before your eyes! Walk in the footsteps of Jesus, stand where King David once stood, and discover the world of Mary of Magdala and countless others. Together, we'll explore the very places where God's story unfolded, bringing the Scriptures to life in ways you've never imagined. Don’t just read the stories—experience them!

Trip Details

We will travel together through the Galilee and Dead Sea regions, stopping at places such as Caesarea Maritima, Mt. Carmel, Megiddo, Capernaum, Masada, and En Gedi. We will even have opportunities to sail on the Sea of Galilee, be baptized in the Jordan River, and float in the Dead Sea!

We will end our incredible journey together in Jerusalem, where we will visit, among other stops, The Garden of Gethsemane, The Temple Mount and Western Wall, and the Garden Tomb. We will even walk on a street where Jesus walked!

  • When: November 5-15, 2025
  • Estimated Cost: $2797 (not including airfare). This includes all tours, in-country transportation, tips, lodging, and some meals.

*We continually monitor the situation in Israel. Although we don’t anticipate this, if necessary, we will postpone until November 6-16, 2026.

For more details, email jamie@theuncommonjourney.net

Footsteps of Paul: Greece and Turkey (Rome Optional)

Join us on an unforgettable journey through the lands where the Apostle Paul and the early Church laid the foundation for Christianity. From the vibrant streets of Istanbul to the breathtaking ruins of Ephesus, this trip will take you through the key sites of Paul’s ministry. Explore the ancient cities of Athens, Corinth, Thessaloniki, Berea, and Philippi, where Paul preached, taught, and established churches that shaped the Christian faith. For those who wish, the adventure can extend to the Eternal City—Rome, where Paul’s journey ultimately led him. This immersive experience offers not just a tour of historical sites but a deep connection to the Scripture and a personal walk through the heart of the early Church.

Trip Details:

Embark on an extraordinary adventure as we trace the Apostle Paul’s journey and explore the world of the early Christians. Our journey begins in Istanbul, Turkey—a vibrant city where ancient history and modern culture collide. From there, we’ll travel to the spectacular ruins of Ephesus, one of the best-preserved ancient cities in the world. Walk through its grand streets, marvel at the impressive Temple of Artemis, and stand in awe of the ancient theater where Paul once preached.

Next, we’ll head to Greece, where the history and beauty of the ancient world come alive. In Athens, walk among the ruins of the Acropolis, where Paul boldly spoke to the philosophers at Mars Hill. In Corinth, stand where Paul spent time teaching and building the early Church, and in Thessaloniki, feel the legacy of Paul’s letters to the church that continues to echo through the ages. We’ll visit Berea, where the people eagerly received the gospel, and Philippi, the site of Paul’s first church in Europe, where the early believers began to shape the future of the Christian faith.

For those who wish, the adventure can extend to the Eternal City—Rome, where Paul was imprisoned and eventually martyred, completing the circle of this life-changing journey. This is more than a trip; it’s an uncommon journey into the heart of Scripture. Don’t miss this unforgettable experience!

  • When: October 7th-October 18th (Rome extension the 18th to 21st)

Walking Alongside: Serve with our Global Missions Partners

Walking Alongside offers two powerful ways for you to engage in God’s work around the world—going and praying—as you partner with our long-time international ministry partners. Whether you’re on the ground, actively serving, or lifting up others through prayer, this journey invites you to be a vital part of what God is doing globally.

Zagreb, Croatia

Fokus’ vision aligns closely with our own—serving God by serving the people of Croatia. Their heart is to make a lasting impact by meeting the spiritual and practical needs of Croatians, all for the glory of God. Through dedicated service, they are walking out their mission to bring hope, encouragement, and the transformative love of Christ to those they serve.

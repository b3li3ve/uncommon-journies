Welcome to Jamie Hulker: Here for You

Where our mission is simple—to serve God by serving people. We believe God’s Word equips and encourages us to grow as disciples of Jesus and live out His call in our lives. Our goal is to make God’s Word accessible while providing meaningful opportunities for us to make a difference in the world together—all for God’s glory. We invite you to join the journey!

About Jamie

Jamie discovered the importance of God's Word, prayer, and Christian community during her college years. Her journey has since taken her to over thirty countries across six continents, where she has served others through evangelism, writing and teaching Bible studies, and various other ministries to encourage spiritual growth. With a deep passion for studying and teaching the Bible from its historical and literary settings, Jamie earned an MA in Ministry from Moody Theological Seminary. Afterward, she founded Jamie Hulker: Here for You to leverage her seasoned experience and education to serve God by equipping and encouraging others to know Him better and follow Him more closely. Jamie lives in Franklin, Tennessee, where she loves to teach the Bible, speak about Jesus, and connect with her community.
